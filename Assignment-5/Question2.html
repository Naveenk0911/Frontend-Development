Welcome to Mathura

Mathura is a sacred city in Uttar Pradesh, India, known as the birthplace of Lord Krishna. It is a city rich in history, culture, and spirituality.

Rich Cultural Heritage

Mathura has been a center of art, music, and dance for centuries. The city celebrates numerous festivals with great enthusiasm, especially Holi and Janmashtami.

Must-Visit Places

Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple, Dwarkadhish Temple, Vishram Ghat, and Govardhan Hill are among the most visited spiritual destinations in Mathura.

Delicious Cuisine

Mathura is famous for its pedas, kachoris, jalebis, and other traditional sweets that attract food lovers from all over India.
